RAVI GHOSH

I write features, profiles and essays on politics and culture. My work—on documentary photography, pop music, British politics and visual art—has appeared in The Guardian, The Economist, Prospect, The White Review, i-D, Verso, the Financial Times, Tribune, the LA Review of Books and many more.

I also work for Elephant magazine, where I cover contemporary art and edit widely across print and digital. I am especially interested in photography’s relationship to place and nation, and mixed-media practices with strong conceptual or sound components. If you are doing interesting work in these areas, feel free to reach out.
