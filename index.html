RAVI GHOSH

I’m a journalist and writer based in London.

My work mainly covers politics and culture, and has appeared in The Guardian, The Economist, Prospect, The White Review, Verso, The Sunday Times, the Financial Times, Exeunt, Huffpost, Dazed and many others.

I also work as an editorial assistant at Elephant magazine, where I write on contemporary art and edit widely across print and digital. For art press, email ravi@elephant.art. Otherwise, it's ravighosh97@gmail.com. You can also find me on Twitter.
