RAVI GHOSH

I’m a journalist and writer based in London.

My work mainly covers politics and culture, and has appeared in The Guardian, The Economist, Prospect, The White Review, Verso, The Sunday Times, the Financial Times, Exeunt, Huffpost, Dazed and many others.
