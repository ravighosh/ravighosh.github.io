RAVI GHOSH

I am a critic and editor based in New York. My writing—on photography, music, politics and the visual arts—has appeared in The Baffler, The Economist, i-D, Verso, RA Magazine, Art Monthly, ArtReview and many more publications. I also write essays for photobooks, artist monographs and galleries.

I am a graduate student in the art history department at Columbia, where my master’s research is funded by university and Fulbright scholarships. Before that, I was deputy editor at the British Journal of Photography in London, and worked for Elephant magazine and the Financial Times.
